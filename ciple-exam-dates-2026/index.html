March 2026 · 5 min read · Updated regularly

Slots fill up fast. CIPLE exam slots in Portugal fill up very quickly, especially in Lisbon. Many candidates report that by spring, the only available dates are in November. Register as early as possible.

How to Register

  1. Go to the CAPLE website: caple.letras.ulisboa.pt
  2. Find available exam sessions and dates
  3. Select your preferred center and date
  4. Fill in registration with personal details
  5. Upload ID (passport, residence permit, or citizen card)
  6. Pay the registration fee (€75-95)
  7. Payment must be completed at least 24 hours before exam
  8. In Portugal: pay by Multibanco, MBWay, or bank transfer

What to Bring on Exam Day

  • Valid ID (same one used to register)
  • Confirmation email (printed or on phone)
  • A pen (blue or black ink)
  • Water bottle
  • Arrive 30 minutes early

How Long Until Results?

Results: approximately 6-7 weeks after the exam. Certificate: another month after results. Total from exam to certificate in hand: 2-3 months. Plan this into your citizenship application timeline.

Tips for Getting a Spot

  1. Check the CAPLE website in January for the full year
  2. Register the day slots open — do not wait
  3. Have your ID and payment ready before registration opens
  4. Consider centers outside Lisbon — Cascais, Setúbal, Coimbra may have spots
  5. Register for the earliest date you can be ready for
  6. Have a backup date in case your first choice fills up

How Long to Prepare?

Most candidates with basic Portuguese from daily life need 4-8 weeks of focused preparation. Starting from scratch? Plan for 3-6 months. Start now, even before registering.
